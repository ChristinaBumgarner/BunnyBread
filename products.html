Bunny Bread Products

Bunny Bread is baked with pride using only the finest ingredients. Eating grain foods, like Bunny Bread, provides many nutrients that are vital for the health and maintenance of our bodies. Grains are important sources of nutrients including several B vitamins (thiamin, riboflavin, niacin, and folate) and minerals (such as iron). Enjoy all of the varieties of Bunny products.
